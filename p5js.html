The "Skrull Shooter" game was my final for Programming for Visual Artists. It's the most complex project I made for that class. It took me hours alone to edit the skull features onto the target images. The game follows you as a new agent of S.H.I.E.L.D. trying to track down alien invaders disguised as the heroes of the Marvel Universe.

The "audio visualizer" was made for an audio-visual project. The parameters of the assignment were that our project had to include both audio and visual elements, so I made an audio visualizer synced to the theme song of Spider-Man: The Animated Series.

Spring 2025: P5js

For my midterm, I made a magic 8 ball of sorts. By clicking on the figure, known as eternity, you will get one of 4 responses. To create the effect of a universe existing in the space of his body, I created a galaxy scene using several effects and animations, then placed an image on top of it. The image is only the white and blue part; the space part is the animation behind him. The voice you hear is my own, just with heavy effects, which I learned during Intro to Sound, as I was taking that class concurrently with Programming for Visual Artists.
