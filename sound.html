When completing my assignments for Intro to Sound, I gave myself a little challenge in addition to the parameters of the assignments. I tried to theme each assignment around one character from Jack Kirby's New Gods series.

Use the second row of buttons to veiw their respective projects

(I recommend lowering your device's volume, as these can be loud.)

spring 2025: audacity

I themed the "INDOOR PROJECT" after Mr. Miracle. The parameters of the assignment were to create sound that dealt with the theme of inside. I thought, who spends more time inside things than an escape artist? I created the unique sounds you hear by adding effects to sounds made using common objects found indoors, such as a sink, a desk, and a box of Tic Tacs.

Spring 2025: audacity

I themed the "voice project" after Orion, the main character of the New Gods saga. We had to manipulate a voice in some way in order to create something new and bold. I used my project to try and tell a story. I interpret it as a signal Orion was sending out for reinforcements in a battle. I made this project by applying filters to my own voice while improvising an alien tongue.

spring 2025: audacity

I themed the "portrait project" around Big Barda. The parameters of the project were to make a sound that was inspired by anonymous answers to a questionnaire. While making sure I kept on task, I infused a small bit of Big Barda's warrior spirit into the sound with the emphasis on percussions and rhythmic beats. As a stoic warrior who is incredibly hard for outsiders to read, she was perfect to slip into a project about interpreting someone through only a small amount of detail.

spring 2025: audacity

I themed the "Glitch project" around Darkseid. The parameters of the assignment were to twist normal audio into something chaotic and unexpected in any way we could. I interpreted this as making something violent and imposing. By theming the project around Darkseid, I could dive into the pain of simply existing on his planet of Apokolips. On that planet there is no rest, only pain, anger, and chaos. I tried my hardest to harness that in pure audio form.
