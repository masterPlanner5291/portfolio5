Spring 2026: Adobe Premier

This editing exercise is the product of a happy accident. A scheduling mishap left me with no help in front of or behind the camera. Left with no other option, I was forced to resort to action figures and clever tricks like camera movement and sharp editing and sound effects added in post to simulate movement and impact. The result is something of a motion comic.

Spring 2026

This is my favorite project I've done for video art so far. I was tasked with creating a video with the theme of disruption. I did this by contrasting one of the most famous and wholesome songs with violence, both mild and intense. This was meant to symbolize all the pain in the world and how we are not given time to process as the media either moves on or distracts us.
